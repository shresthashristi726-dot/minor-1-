About Us

R J Dhaka Collection is a leading business-to-business provider of high-quality Dhaka products in Nepal. Established in 2060, we have been dedicated to bringing the timeless beauty and craftsmanship of Dhaka textiles to the Nepali market. Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source our Dhaka products directly from renowned artisans in Bangladesh, ensuring that every piece meets our stringent standards. From the finest fabrics to intricate embroidery, we believe in offering only the best to our valued customers. Whether you're a retailer looking to expand your product line or a designer seeking unique materials, R J Dhaka Collection is your trusted partner. Discover the elegance and durability of Dhaka products and elevate your business with our exceptional offerings.
